ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

A Kingdom Blueprint For Wealth

Kingdom Finance Conference 2026 is a four-week immersion into the financial wisdom of Scripture, built for believers who are done managing scarcity and ready to steward abundance.

Our Mission

To equip individuals with spiritual wisdom, financial intelligence, and practical tools through teaching, mentorship, and real-world insights that promote personal growth, wealth creation, and responsible stewardship.

Our Vision

To raise financially empowered, purpose-driven believers who understand biblical principles, develop practical skills, and create lasting impact in their communities and the marketplace.

OUR CORE OBJECTIVES

Equip

Provide practical knowledge and tools for personal and financial growth.

Empower

Help participants identify opportunities and develop wealth-creating skills.

Transform

Build mindsets that lead to responsible stewardship and lasting impact.

Connect

Create an environment for learning, collaboration, and mentorship.

Inspire

Encourage participants to pursue excellence and purpose in every area of life.
