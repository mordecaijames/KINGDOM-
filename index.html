ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

A Kingdom Blueprint For Wealth

Kingdom Finance Conference 2026 is a four-week immersion into the financial wisdom of Scripture, built for believers who are done managing scarcity and ready to steward abundance.

Our Purpose

To equip the Body of Christ in Kaduna and beyond with practical, biblically-grounded financial intelligence, bridging the gap between faith and the marketplace.

Our Vision

A generation of believers who fund the Gospel, build sustainable businesses, and walk in financial dominion without compromising Kingdom values.

The Impact

Across four transformative sunday's, attendees will gain frameworks for budgeting, investing, business growth, and Kingdom-aligned wealth, taught by seasoned ministers and marketplace leaders.
